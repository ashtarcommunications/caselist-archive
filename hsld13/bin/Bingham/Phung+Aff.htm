In addition, there are four theoretical justifications for using Utilitarianism:

  1. Ground – Utilitarianism is the most fair criterion for the round because nearly every advantage can fall under Util, including those that are Deontological in nature; it allows both debaters in the round equal ground to win under it in the Framework debate.
    2. Predictability – Util is one of the most commonly run Frameworks in LD, and it is the easiest to be able to conform to during the round for the opposite debater, making it the most fair.
    3.
