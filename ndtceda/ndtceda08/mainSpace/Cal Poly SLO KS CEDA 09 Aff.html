Act 1: Is the Matrix

Debate that has constantly dwelled in this simulacrum of change where we believe we can pass legislation but at the end of the round nothing takes place. It’s a facsimile where we are re-set by the machines and then the system, the pairings dictate where we go next and what we argue. We are neg one round and then aff another but there is no purpose. We may gain education, but that’s the education of the system that we have been entrenched in from the start.
