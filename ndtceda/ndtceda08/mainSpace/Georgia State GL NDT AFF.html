Definitions:

1. Foreign assistance is defined by the foreign assistance act of 1961. GAO 95

2. This includes Development assistance, food aid, disaster relief, humanitarian iad, peacekeeping forces and security assistance. Moore 01

3. Including means containing as a subordinate element - therefore the affirmative must include more than just a security guarantee and foreign assistance - it must also include diplomatic action separate from the aid process, ie the constructive engagement portion of the plan.

Violations:

1. Conflict mediation is diplomatic activity, not foreign assistance. We did a search of the 500-plus page foreign assistance act and the phrase conflict mediation doesn’t appear in it - we will enter a screenshot of that search into the record for the debate - and our interpretation establishes that it is the aff’s burden to prove that their plan falls under the provisions of the act - not our burden to prove that it doesn’t - such is the nature of a prima facie burden of proof

2. the plan says that they give aid between two actors - it doesn’t give it to any actor in the topic - something which is between two objects is not on or towards either of them by definition
