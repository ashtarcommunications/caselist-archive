CHICKEN


HARAWAY 2007

(Donna, When Species Meet, “Chicken”, University of Minnesota Press, 2007, Chapter 10 - pg. 265-274, She is a professor and chair of the History of Consciousness Program at the University of California, Santa Cruz,)


Our affirmative is a critique of the economic and technological rationale of the resolution's orientation towards agricultural support. We defend the reading of the 1AC as a way to reorient ourselves towards the commodities in the resolution, critiquing the objectification of these commodities. Our impacts include eugenics, biopower, and technoscience. We feel our affirmative is a prerequisite to any good policy option that deals with how we support agriculture in the United States. Our framework is that debates should be about finding the best methodologies for knowledge production.
