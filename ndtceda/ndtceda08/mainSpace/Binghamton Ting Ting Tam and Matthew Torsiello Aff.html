We begin our process of affirmation by engaging the metaphor of the monoculture. Monocultures of nature and Monocultures of the mind: when, in the course of creating value based on sameness, reiteration of norms, and the goals of the market, there is a loss of diversity- both of ways of thinking and of the biodiversity that creates our shared material existence. This form of thinking is what justifies the either/or logic necessary for ecocide and genocide. We must break free from this form of thinking by rejecting the policies and activities that maintain their justification.
