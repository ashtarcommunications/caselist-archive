Observation Three: Dreampolitik
[] We believe the plan should happen even if status quo political realities mean it is a fantasy. The concept of fiat was not introduced to encourage debates over whether the current composition of the Congress is most amenable to pushing the legislation; it was intended to eliminate “workability” debates because those arguments disable the imaginative and progressive potential of policy debate.
