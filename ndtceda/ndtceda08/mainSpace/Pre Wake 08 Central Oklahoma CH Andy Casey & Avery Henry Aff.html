Metaphysics operates according to a binary logic that sets two terms of reality: a something and a nothing. The bountiful field is the something of agriculture (the identity which land must conform to) whereas the desert is the nothing of the resolution and agriculture. metaphysics informs the agricultural policy of subsidies and thus makes it complicit with the civilizing mission to master the continent and make it productive. because the desert does not concede to the demand to be productive (it is unable to be accommodated). American policy responds by banishing the desert (evidence indicates this is why we respond so acceptingly to test weapons in the desert). The affirmative undertakes what is called a de-structive genealogy of the term "agriculture" agri - from agrios (the wild or savage) and culture - from cultivate/acculturation (a cognate of colonus).

the response to this metaphysical inquiry is to think the (trope of the desert as vast unproductive nothingness) positively by affirming the topical exile (which in the context of agriculture is the desert). This response subverts metaphysical imperialism by de-sedimenting its hold on how reality ought function.

Western thought has a historical tendency of refusing to question its own interpretation of reality. Affirmations of the topic have traditionally responded to the topical hail by affirming an instrumental change in policy. Traditional views of the topic attempt uniformity in the meanings of resolutions. Every word is set to have a specific usage that makes clear the meaning behind the resolution. This interpretation is set to exclude alternative ways of viewing the topic and reality.
However, there is meaning behind the words that cannot be interpreted solely by the writers of the topic. Each word cannot be isolated from the rest of the group. They are all connected to each other to form meaning. Each person has a different meaning that the resolution speaks to and it comes to them in different ways whether they are literal, figurative, historical, and a host of others. Our affirmation of the resolution is not a minor repair to the usual system: rather, we affirm an entirely different interpretation of reality. This is Vine Deloria Jr. in 1999
