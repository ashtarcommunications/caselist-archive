TERRORISM AFF:

We start with a performance of a narration and display of a terrorist and affirm constructive engagement with the terrorists of the five topic countries. We use Joseph Tuman's definition of terrorism, a means to create anxiety and spark conversation, to set up a non-traditional dialogical framework for the debate. Using Julia Kristeva's description of revolution as a paradigm of always questioning and Mark Edelman Boren's discussion of the important role of student revolutions, we say revolution should be activated in places like the classroom, and that our 1ac is the ignition of an educational revolution.
