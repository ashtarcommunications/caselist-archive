The 1ac makes a fatal attempt at understanding the global predicament and particularly the middle east arrangements. History has many stories to be told of colonialism, imperialisms, nationalism and, now we contest neoliberal globalization. The perception that those in the Middle East will have of the affirmative plan must investigated beyond the talking heads of the political journals or media that proclaims “X” country will accept our plan. Our position is that the affirmative engagement is focused on political and economic engagement, but does not investigate the importance of cultural engagement. Affirmative policies on the economical and political level are locked up in the ideology of neoliberal globalization that cannot be separated from the state actors by which they endorse.
