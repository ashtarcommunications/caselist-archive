Contention III: Solvency


The plan text accompanied by the year 2011 is a symbolic representation that proves other historical narratives exist, which then disrupts the linear time and space of linear politics that strive for a future outcome. Anthropolgists say that we do not recognize a power structure until it is in the process of being deconstructed. Therefore, the 1AC serves as a fluid historical artifact that builds upon a constellation of intersecting histories and narratives that challenge the securitized and governmentality discourses that shape the border and body politic by complicating accepted truth systems, especially in the context of biomedicine and securitization.
