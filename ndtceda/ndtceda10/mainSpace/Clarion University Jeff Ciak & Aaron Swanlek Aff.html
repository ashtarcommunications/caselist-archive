Therefore, an undocumented immigrant will only come forward if her employment situation is so desperate or dangerous that she is willing to risk removal based only on the chance that she will be granted relief. At the same time, employers can minimize their risks by keeping their employees satisfied with their employment conditions. n231 Under this incentive structure, the mere option of cooperation between employees and law enforcement forces an employer to choose: either abide by immigration laws to avoid employer sanctions, or treat employees well enough so that they do not become informants. Though this may limit criminal prosecutions, it is by no means a reason to reject this Note's proposal. While not the same as full lawful status, employees benefit from the mere prospect of increased enforcement.
