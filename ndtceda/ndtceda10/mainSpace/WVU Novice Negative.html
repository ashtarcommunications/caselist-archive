Contention 3: The Solution
The Alternative
Our alternative we endorse is the abandonment of our belief in capitalism. Though not ideal, rejection of docile political action within the prevailing capitalist system is the only means to effectively solve for capitalism and poverty in the long run. Our individual rejection has substantial transformative potential because capitalism’s existence is sustained only by the willingness of individuals to believe in the system, and this desertion is the first step to initiating a new socio-political reality.
Johnson 2004
Adrian <Prof Emory University> “The Cynic’s Fetish: Slavoj Zizek and the dynamics of belief” Psychoanalysis, Culture and Society”
Our alternative may not offer citizens a direct, concrete action, the first crucial step towards changing things for the better requires a liberation of thinking and the self-discipline to resist the temptation of acting hastily on concrete plans that are doomed to fail.
