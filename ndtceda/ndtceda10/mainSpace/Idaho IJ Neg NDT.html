Deterrence K - Soapbox


“Torture” is to “enhanced interrogation” as “deterrence” is to “the constant threat of nuclear annihilation”

The discourse of “deterrence” makes weapons seem defensive and removes debate over the existence of nuclear weapons, making the only relevant question if deterrence “works” – the linguistic move provides the elites ultimate ‘top cover’ backing world-wide wars of aggression to sustain a global empire.

The result is that dominant opinion among experts and political leaders generates policy debate viewed historically is absurdly dangerous. Humanity can not long survive a global order of things in which “terror without end” lies at the center of power, with those who rule most of us in most places still deploying limitless violence to keep things as they are.

Its necessary to confront deterrence for what it is, even if our substitutes are clunky, like “constant- nuclear-weapons-threat.”
