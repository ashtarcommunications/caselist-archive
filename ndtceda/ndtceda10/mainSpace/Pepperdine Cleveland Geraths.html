Alternative: We offer a swamping of psychological borders through the Mestiza Consciousness, and a re-education of pedagogy produced within the public sphere by a critical examination of “Our Narratives.” The Mestiza consciousness is fluid and exists outside of normative perspectives on society. It offers a new lens to view the world, one that is inclusionary rather than exclusionary

Arguments within the 1NC:

1) Critique of the resolution.
2) Critique of dualistic system of borders in society using Anzaldua's notions of the Borderlands and the Mestiza Consciousness.
Anzaldua (Gloria, Chicana Activist, Writer, Former Professor, Borderlands: The New Mestiza/La Frontera (Third Edition)). 2004
3) Discussion of the oppressive nature of "passing." (Taken from Sloop 2004)

4) Discussion of the ways in which knowledge produced in the round and outside the round is important to embodying our advocacy.
Giroux (Professor of English and Cultural Studies at McMaster University) 1991
