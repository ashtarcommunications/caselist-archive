Slightly shorter version of Benedict & Maerowitz's aff.

Plan: The United States Congress should amend the Immigration and Nationality Act to substantially expand the eligibility for employment-based visas by exempting from the numerical caps for permanent resident cards the following persons: (1) all foreign-born students earning a Master’s degree or a Ph.D. in a field of science, technology, engineering or mathematics (STEM) from an accredited U.S. University; (2) all foreign-born professionals with H-1B visa status having a Master’s or Ph.D. in a STEM field, including their spouses and children.
