GSU Pre-Tournament Disclosure

AFGHANISTAN AFF

Plan:
The United States Federal Government should offer a security guarantee to the government of Afghanistan providing that the American military will protect Afghanistan against internal threats to stability and against invasion by outside powers. The United States Federal Government should provide ten billion dollars in reconstruction aid to the government of Afghanistan. Such aid levels should be maintained on an annual basis as long as Afghanistan makes verifiable progress in the areas of good governance and judicial reform. Distribution of aid should occur through provincial reconstruction teams.
