New Iran 1NC: (John Carroll / Miami of Ohio / Northwestern)


Text:
The United States Federal Government should engage in a military campaign against the Government of Iran for the purpose of regime change. The United States Federal Government should utilize an effects based strategy, targeting critical infrastructure, including electricity, communications, transportation, military, and industrial nodes. The United States Federal Government should also target Iranian nuclear facilities and infrastructures, including bridges and tunnels. The United States should also target Iranian gasoline refineries as well as gasoline storage facilities. The United States should not use nuclear bunker buster nor nuclear weapons during these strikes. The United States Federal Government should deploy all necessary forces to the Gulf of Oman to declare and enforce a halt of all shipments of Iranian oil and guarantee the safety of tankers and other platforms carrying non Iranian oil. The United States Federal Government should send special operation forces to seize key Iranian oil assets, including a series of 100 offshore wells and platforms built on Iran’s continental shelf, as well as the North and South Pars offshore fields. The United States should support ethnic resistance groups within Iran, including the Kurds, Balochs, and Azeris, both logistically and financially, in order to turn sections of the country into regional autonomous zones. Funding and enforcement guaranteed
