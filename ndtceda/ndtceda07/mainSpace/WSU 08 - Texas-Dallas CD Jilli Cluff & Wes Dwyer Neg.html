Environmental Managerialism K

The politics of the 1AC rely on expert knowledge that presupposes an objective and quantifiable understanding of the environment. Scientific and technocratic ecological disciplines however, are a product of a specific sociopolitical location whose ultimate goal is the complete mastery of nature. This expert knowledge legitimates and expands capitalist and biopolitical control of the environment, turning the case.
