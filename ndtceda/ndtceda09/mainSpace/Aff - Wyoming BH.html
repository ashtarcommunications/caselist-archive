Through its ordering power the state is able to control and restrict all communiation and information regarding nuclear weapons. In fact all data relating to nuclear weapons is “born secret”, even relating a dream about nuclear weapons endangers national security and is against the law, it places the individual outside the statist grid of ordering
Morland in 2005
(Howard, journalist and activist, “Born Secret”, CARDOZO LAW REVIEW, VOL 26, NO 4, MARCH 2005, PP. 1401-8, http://www.fas.org/sgp/eprint/cardozo.pdf -rkc)

The control of information about nuclear weapons and the ability to organize and striate society freezes the social and maintains a society of perfect control, justified by the fear of nuclear weapons and the state security apparatus.

Dillon, 2008 (Michael, “Revisiting Franco’s Death” Foucault on Politics, Security and War. Pg 167-169, mb)
