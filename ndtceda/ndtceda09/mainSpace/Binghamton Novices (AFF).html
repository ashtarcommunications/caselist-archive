NOVICE AFF #3

We propose that the United States Federal Government should immediately implement a sizable and effective anti-ballistic missile defense system on United States Soil and that the United States should reduce the role of the reflexive Nuclear Deterrent.
The United States has been able to deploy an effective anti-Ballistic Missile Defense System, hereafter referred to as ABM, since the early 1960s with the concurrent development of the SAFEGUARD and SENTINEL systems which provided protection to U.S. Cities and Ballistic Missile silos. Currently the United States is developing the THAAD, or Terminal High Altitude Area Defense, system. This system has proven itself in a series of test runs against simulated ICBMs and has even proven itself as capable to make smart in flight changes to its targeting profile. This means that if deployed over a wide area of the United States it would be able to effectively target and shoot down small waves of Nuclear armed missiles and save civilian lives as a result.
This would mean that accidental wars started over misread radar signatures, flocks of geese or accidentally fired missiles would no longer be possible as the decision to rely on the ABM system could be made to wait out the perceived attacks, or to destroy the single missile. Not only could this save millions, if not billions, of lives from any accidental war but it eases Nuclear tensions as well because the United States would no longer rely on launching its Nuclear ICBM force in retaliation to all such attacks launched on its soil. Because the U.S. would no longer have its Nuclear force on full alert Russia, China, France and the U.K. would no longer need to fear a U.S. Nuclear attack and could reduce their force simultaneously to account for this.
