The resolution’s invocation of America’s nuclear arsenal and posture is like every debater’s dream, drawing a quick straight line to extinction scenarios. For once, this tendency is not unique to debaters, because nuclear weapons in our collective imagination have become significant only as agents of Armageddon, some future scenario of fantastic apocalypse looming overhead.
