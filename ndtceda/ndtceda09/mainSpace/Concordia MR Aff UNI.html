We utilize Euripides’ play of Iphigenia and Agamemnon (simply called Iphigenia of Aulis) in relation to the Orientalism that is inherent Obama’s policy of disarmament which we claim as being rooted in American Exceptionalism. Using Iphigenia’s tactic of repetition to expose the contradiction of US imperialism, we claim the Iranians for Peace embark on such a strategy.
