From time to time its necessary to remind this community about its false side. Behind its liberal façade is an unquestioned loyalty to a status quo that is not real. Iran, CTBT, dealert, corn subsidies, wind energy, pressuring china, constructively engaging Syria: all of these issues from time to time are blamed as a locus for causing a nuclear war. But what is a nuclear war and who does a nuclear war happen to? The problem with contemporary aff’s obsessive need to locate nuclear wars is a complete avoidance of the questions of culture, race, and how the government relates to identity through the channel of nuclear policies. Well its time to remind this community about what actually happens under the guise of nuclear war.
