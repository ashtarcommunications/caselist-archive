Our aff is written and performed in a narrative format that would be difficult to transfer here so I am giving a brief explanation of it. If you have any further questions, feel free to e-mail me at michael_ely16@yahoo.com.

We start the discussion of the affirmative in reference to another round where everyone was masturbating. This opens a discussion for production versus anti-production where we compare masturbation as an anti-productive act that doesn't yield intensities or creativity as opposed to sex which produces connections and breaks. We continue the discussion by stating that these debaters where just building a story tracing the existence of the united states' nuclear arsenal in a metonymic relationship to the social sphere (socius). We say that that structure is a bad form of politics because it plans out the future making a genealogy. This structure is only taken into account in the external, we say that we should have a micropolitical politics of desire. Our method is schizoanalysis. We also put in a discussion of the fear of death. We say that there is not a distinction between life and death, but rather we are in a constant stage of becoming. We perceive the 1ac as a minor text that challenges the dominant narrative of the nuclear arsenal.
