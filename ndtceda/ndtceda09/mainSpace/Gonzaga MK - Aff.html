Contention One: the Status Quo

Status quo declaratory is still locked in the Reagan Administration’s desire to achieve unassailability – the Nuclear Posture review is the epitome of the attempt to lock the United States in as the shining city on a hill by attempting to coerce “rogue” states into succumbing to the United States security agenda through threats of nuclear first strike.
Huntley 4 (Wade, Program Director at the Simons Centre for Disarmament and Non-Proliferation Research in the Liu Institute for Global Studies at the University of British Columbia, “Unthinking the Unthinkable: U.S. Nuclear Policy and Asymmetric Threats,” Strategic Insights, Volume II, Issue 2, February)
We will use our nuclear weapons and the threat of their use as a means of preserving the so-called “Western Way of Life” ignoring the millions of people abroad that would die as a result of our nuclear brinksmaship
