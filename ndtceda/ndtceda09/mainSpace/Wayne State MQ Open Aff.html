Contention One: God Mode

The United States has come to see its nuclear arsenal as a new God, able to protect us as long as we believe in It. This belief has produced a dichotomy that constructs the US as absolute good and our “enemies” as absolute evil. This mindset allows us to strive for omnipotence at any cost, which necessarily dehumanizes our Enemy and allows us to use any means necessary to destroy it.
Chernus, ’86 (Ira, Professor of Religious Studies at the University of Colorado at Boulder, Dr. Strangegod: On the Symbolic Meaning of Nuclear Weapons, p. 32-35)

By starting with the observation … we ever be truly free?

Omnipotence would be great if it was possible, but it’s not. In fact, our attempt to maintain nuclear superiority leads to increased militarization which makes proliferation and war inevitable.
