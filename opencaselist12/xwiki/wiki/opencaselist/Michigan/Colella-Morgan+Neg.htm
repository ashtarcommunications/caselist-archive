Wu added that Sany had notified CFIUS about the Colorado investment and that the committee had no objections to the transaction.

Informal consultations

Traditionally, one way for foreign companies to avoid CFIUS jurisdiction is to invest through companies held by US citizens.
In such cases, executives from the acquirer would not take a seat on the board of the target company or have a say in corporate decisionmaking, Jacobs said, but added that many companies are not willing to sacrifice that level of control.
