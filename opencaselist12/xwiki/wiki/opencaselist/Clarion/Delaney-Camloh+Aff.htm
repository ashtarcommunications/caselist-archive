Plan

The Department of Energy should revise 10 C.F.R. PART 810 governing deemed exports for the production of nuclear energy by:

  1. Extending the current list of generally authorized nations and add China, India, Kazakhstan, Russia, Ukraine, and the United Arab Emirates as generally authorized nations
    2. Define citizenship for foreign nationals as their most recent citizenship or permanent residency
    3. Limit jurisdiction to the transfer by any person subject to the jurisdiction of the United States of any technology that is required for the production of any special nuclear material to any foreign person
    4. Establish expedited review with binding deadlines for specific authorizations.
