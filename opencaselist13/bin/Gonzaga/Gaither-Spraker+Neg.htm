City of Northglenn 11 (City of Northglenn Zoning Ordinance, “Rules of Construction – Definitions”, http://www.northglenn.org/municode/ch11/content_11-5.html)

Section 11-5-3. Restrictions. As used in this Chapter 11 of the Municipal Code, the term
AND
operation, building, structure or thing shall be authorized by any permit or license.

Decreasing authority requires reducing the permission to act, not the ability to act

Taylor 96 (Ellen, Attorney at Gambrell and Stolz, LLP, Atlanta, 21 Del. J. Corp. L. 870 (1996), Hein Online)

The term authority is commonly thought of in the context of the law of agency, and the
AND
what the agent can do and what the agent may do.
