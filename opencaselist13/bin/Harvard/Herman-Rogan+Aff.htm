aaa NOTES ON WIKI ORGANIZATION

Tournament: Gsu | Round: 1 | Opponent: Houston Jennings-Jennings | Judge: Koch
Because the wiki auto-alphabetizes everything, I'm attempting to implement a pseudo-organization scheme such that the 2AC blocks from different affs don't get mixed up over the course of the year.  As it stands, everything relating to the CIA-DOD aff read at GSU begins with a z.  With each successive aff, the letter code will decreased by one so that the most relevant cites appear near the top of the wiki page.

2ACs on case and 1AR cards are grouped by round rather than argument to avoid clutter.  Everything else is grouped by argument.  As new cards make their way into 2AC blocks, the relevant wiki entries will be updated so the most recent version always appears on this page.
