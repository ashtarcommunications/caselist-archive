Notes from Justin GreenPlease recognize this is a flow of what happened NOT what Towson themselves has admittedPerception flowability etc should all be factored into this scouting

Form of the 1ac
- It is an embodied message of the value of strong loud proud and rightfully angry black women about the state of affairs in America and the world
- Extremely poetic and passionate  There are no "cards" read  If this were a poetry slam the snaps would be thunderous
- In the cross-ex it was revealed that they were using the words of several authors and had them footnoted but not read aloud  (Footnotes to the 1ac attached)  They provided them immediately upon request
- Several black women are listed who have suffered  I was unable to gather the names of allSome of the stories indicated situations of solemness solidarity and support (a 7 year old who dies a mother jailed who shot her abusive husband) others indicate both 

Thesismessage obtained about the 1ac (my interpretationsobservations)
- we are the reducers of the war powers of the president that have been shaped by stereotypes of black womenWe do not advocate for a statutory or judicial or USFG action
- War is a very broad concept - it is far beyond nations fighting - war is also poverty racism discrimination violence in the every dayConceptions about war that assume nations fighting ignorecondone violence of the every day against black women
- The 1ac is an act of self-love  This self love is critical to stop black women from having self-hatred which prevents them from being effective political agents
- I have no doubt they have made arguments in other debates -

Cross Ex questions and Answers I have flowed
Q What is the sapphire
A (This answer changed from round 1 to round 6 to quarters)  It varied from "the image of the strong black woman" to "anyone who resists" to 

Q What Presidential war powers do you reduce
A1 We reduce the stereotype of the irrational black female that undergirds all of war powers
A2 We reduce the war powers used by the President against black women

Q What is the role of the ballot
A To vote for the team with the best scholarship
Q Do you think the ballot has any spill-over effects is it symbolic
A It might but we think the judge should vote for the team with the best scholarship "we aren't trying to link to your ballot commodification claims" - vote for us if we have good scholarship - if not - vote against us - we aren't trying to fix debate - this is about us in this debate
