A small preface to what we're doing here:
We've decided that Word documents are the best way to post many cites on the wiki while preserving organization and accessibility. We post the most recent 1AC directly on the wiki with partial text (albeit small-sized for organization), then we post a Word document with full text for the 1AC and each additional subheading. We also provide a short description of opponent, affirmative, 1NC strategies, block division, 2NR choice, and decision from all of our negative rounds. After that, we post 2NR statistics. We try to put up the full text of every 1NC, 1AC, and 2AC card that we read. The only exception to this rule is 1NC case arguments - to upload all of those would be quite difficult. We will put up cards we've read in any other speech in any debate by request. Naturally, we're only human, so we probably will forget some cites along the way. Feel free to email us (Milan generally manages most disclosure) if you need any help finding anything, want us to put up more cites, or have trouble accessing anything here. If one of us doesn't respond promptly, try the other.

Negative questions: email Meyer at .
