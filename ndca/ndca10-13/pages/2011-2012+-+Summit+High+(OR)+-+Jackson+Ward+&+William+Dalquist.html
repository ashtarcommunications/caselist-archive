*United States of Amerikkka off the Planet.*
The desk you are sitting in rests on stolen land. No matter where you go and what you do, millions were killed and their blood rests in the ground beneath our feet.
If we want to come to grips with reality. We must begin by rethinking our relationship to the Western ontological predisposition to colonize and destroy.
Even if there is a such thing as “beneficial imperialism,” those benefits are more accidental than causal.
The inherent contradiction of the Western Metanarrative permits the atrocities of Enlightenment and Manifest Destiny by rationalizing the benefits. It is this complacency, however, which makes global violence both possible and inevitable
Spanos, Distinguished Professor of English and comparative literature at Binghamton University “America's shadow: an anatomy of empire,” 2000
Only by giving back the land and rethinking our relationship to this colonization as THE starting point to oppression can we solve the classism, racism, sexism, and militarism which make violence and extinction inevitable
Ergo the plan text: The United States should relocate itself into outer space there by ceding the land back to the First People.
Lack of decolonization will result in our eventual extinction it is try or die for the affirmative.
*Nuclear Waste Aff*
Contention One: Inherency
NASA has abandoned nuclear waste disposal in space.
Unfortunately, this means that nuclear waste is rapidly piling up—no other solution has been found.
Plan
The United States federal government should direct the National Aeronautics and Space Administration and the Department of Energy to develop laser propulsion technology that disposes of nuclear waste materials into Venus’s orbit.
Contention Two: Harms
The USFG and the DOE consistently has been storing its nuclear waste on impoverished Native American communities. –There is no debate here.
Nuclear testing and storage is an ongoing nuclear war against indigenous people and the third world.
This Nuclear War has lead to a literal genocide of the indigenous peoples
Also, the killing of Native Americans makes all other killing possible.
Furthermore :
Neutron radiation from increased nuclear activity has empirically increased seismic activity, this means nuclear waste can literally cause the earth to explode.
Contention Three: Solvency
The U.S. is critical to getting other countries off the reprocessing boat
Laser propulsion is the BEST METHOD for staffed space exploration.
The plan solves – laser propulsion technology is effective__, __safe__, and __cheaper than geological disposal.

We pedagogically prioritize purpose allowing epistemological growth to ALTER the social structure. Space is the unknown that offers the potential of unlimited benefits a chance to start over once we have exhausted the possibilities of exploitation that are harnessed within our grasp.

Charles Mills, Associate Prof of Philosophy @ U Illinois, 1997, Chicago The Racial Contract; p. 1-3

Dr. Reid-Brinkley, Shanara Rose, Professor of African American Studies at Pittsburg University, 2008 “The Harsh Realities of ‘Acting Black’”: How African America Policy Debaters Negotiate Representation Through Racial Performance And Style, C.A.]
