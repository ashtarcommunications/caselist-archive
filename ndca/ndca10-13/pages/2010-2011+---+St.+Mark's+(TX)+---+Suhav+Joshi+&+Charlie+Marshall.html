Beijing's failure to rein in an increasingly reckless Pyongyang is a legitimate security issue for the United States. So too are China's breathtakingly broad claims in the South China Sea. It is unrealistic to expect, for example, that China will incur the risks involved in pressuring North Korea if the United Statesoffers nothing in return. If we want China to take on serious risks to tame North Korea, we have to offer some incentives.
