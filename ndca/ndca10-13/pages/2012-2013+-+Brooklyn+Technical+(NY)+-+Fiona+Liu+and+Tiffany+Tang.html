Contention 1: Transport and the body
Transportation infrastructure carries with it a history of spatial exclusion and violence. America itself is a historical battleground for the controlling of populations via transporting marked bodies and the ghettoization of those deemed lesser. From the slave trade transporting Africans to America, to the trail of tears pushing natives to the periphery, to the exploitation of Chinese rail road workers; this same violence is integral to the smooth functioning oppressive structures. Begin your analysis with how bodies are destroyed in the spaces they live in.
