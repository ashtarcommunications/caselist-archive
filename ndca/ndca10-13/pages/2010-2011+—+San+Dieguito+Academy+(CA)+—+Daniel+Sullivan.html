Affirmative

My Value for this round is Morality.
Since Ought implies a moral obligation Morality is the best fitting value from the resolution.
My Standard for this round is Saving Lives.
Saving lives is Moral so we ought to do it.
Contention 1: Spending.


MONEY SPENT ON NUCLEAR WEAPONS COULD BE SPEND ELSEWHERE TO SAVE LIVES IN THE STATUS QUO.
The International Peace Bureau. International Peace Bureau. DON'T SPEND MONEY ON WAR – USE IT FOR PEACE!. 2007.

Negative



My value for this round is Utilitarianism

Utilitarianism is a way to weigh impacts. It allows debaters to decide what we ought and ought not to do based on the consequences of each of those actions.

The only way we can weigh these impacts within utilitarianism is by weighing lives so my Standard for this round is Saving Lives
