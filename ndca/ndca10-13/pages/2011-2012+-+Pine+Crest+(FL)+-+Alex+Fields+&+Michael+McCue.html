The sole purpose of the ballot is to answer the resolutional question: Is the outcome of the enactment of a topical plan better than the status quo or a competitive policy option?

1) Fairness --- the grammar of the resolution is decisively policy-oriented

The Proposition of Policy:… future action that you propose.

This undermines the singular logical purpose of debate: the search for the best policy. Logical policymaking is the biggest educational impact --- any other learning is worthless because it can’t be applied to the real world.

A) Time and strategy skew- not letting the affirmative frame and weigh their affirmative moots the entire 1ac- it puts the aff at a structural disadvantage as we have dedicated 8 minutes to our advocacy and the negative would get one more speech then us

Discursive analysis can more easily be integrated into a debate about “the USFG should” than questions of what the USFG should do can be integrated into a debate about discourse- we allow them their kritik as long as they run it as a reason why the plan is bad
