Contention 1: Impacts

Extinction is inevitable –
Overpopulation
CBD 9 [Center for Biological Diversity (“Overpopulation and Extinction”, 2009 is the last date cited under the article,http://www.biologicaldiversity.org/campaigns/overpopulation/extinction/index.html]Extinction is the most serious, utterly irreversible effect of human
overpopulation
Asteroids, warming and super-viruses
Monga Bay 6 [environmental science and conservation news site, 2006 (November 30. “To avoid extinction humans must colonize space says Hawking.” http://news.mongabay.com/2006/1130-hawking.html]"Life on Earth is at the ever-increasing risk of being wiped out by a disaster,

Gamma ray bursts
Ottawa Citizen 2k[December 30, “Apocalypse how: Hollywood and doomsday cults have long envisioned the end of the world. On the eve of 2001, Corey S. Powell looks at 20 ways Armageddon could really happen.”, Lexis/]The potent radiation would cook the atmosphere, creating nitrogen oxides that would destroy the ozone layer
Rogue black holes
Ottawa Citizen continues[December 30, “Apocalypse how: Hollywood and doomsday cults have long envisioned the end of the world. On the eve of 2001, Corey S. Powell looks at 20 ways Armageddon could really happen.”, Lexis/]Our galaxy is full of black holes

Solar storms
Ottawa Citizen continues[December 30, “Apocalypse how: Hollywood and doomsday cults have long envisioned the end of the world. On the eve of 2001, Corey S. Powell looks at 20 ways Armageddon could really happen.”, Lexis/]Within a few hours, a superflare on the sun could fry Earth
Magnetic polarity reverse
Ottawa Citizen continues[December 30, “Apocalypse how: Hollywood and doomsday cults have long envisioned the end of the world. On the eve of 2001, Corey S. Powell looks at 20 ways Armageddon could really happen.”, Lexis/]Earth's magnetic field dwindles almost to nothing
Super-volcanoes
Armageddon Online 2 [11/5/2, “Yellowstone is a supervolcano overdue for eruption”, http://www.rense.com/general31/overdue.htm, [CL] eruption could truly be an extinction level event.
War is inevitable – evolution
Thayer 2k [Bradley A., Former Research Fellow, International Security Program, Associate Professor of Defense & Strategic Study, Missouri State University, International Security, 01622889, Fall2000, Vol. 25, Issue 2 “"Bringing in Darwin: Evolutionary Theory, Realism, and International Politics"] victories of the original band "might propel the spread of the genes
Humanity has lots of ways of killing itself or going extinct – space colonization would solve ALL of them.
Falconi, Physicist,1981 (THE CASE FOR SPACE COLONIZATION, http://nutri.com/space/) in our ignorance we should take urgent steps to protect man's future and proceed with the colonization of space immediately.
And, key timeframe is the next five years—otherwise space ventures will collapse
Cordell 8 [PhD planetary and space physics, Weizmann postdoctoral fellow at Caltech, ex-physics professor at the California State University, , worked with NASA. May 11 08 (Bruce, "Nasa programs and MEPs" **Error! Hyperlink reference not valid.**] the most important issue for humanity’s future – within the next 5 – 10 years — is to resume the large-scale human expansion into space
And, Space solves environmental degradation
The Economist 7 (September 29, “Spacemen are from Mars; Sputnik”, L/N, accessed on 7/14/08) the biggest mental change wrought by spaceflight has been not an appreciation of the vastness of the universe, but rather of the smallness, fragility and unity of Earth

Solves asteroids
Dyson 5 (Science Historian and Son of Freeman Dyson, who was one of the original three scientists who worked on Project Orion (George, “The Grandest Rocket Ever.”, February, Volume. 26, Issue 2, Academic Search Complete) on standby to deflect meteors or other objects that threaten Earth.
And, even small asteroids devastate the Ozone layer
Bekey 9 (Ivan, International Academy of Astronautics, “Dealing with the THREAT TO EARTH From ASTEROIDS and COMETS”,http://iaaweb.org/iaa/Scientific%20Activity/Study%20Groups/SG%20Commission%203/sg35/sg35finalreport.pdf) the ozone layer might be largely destroyed by NEOs
Extinction
Williams 96 [David Crockett Williams, Jr., author of Tetron Natural Unified Field Theory, Chemist, Personal and Financial Agent. February 7, 1996 THE SCIENTIFIC SPIRITUAL REVOLUTIONhttp://www.angelfire.com/on/GEAR2000/video96.html] ozone layer depletion that threatens all life
No risk of defense – asteroids cause mass extinctions – over 1000 potential collisions
Easterbrook 5 [Gregg, Senior editor at The New Republic, Daily Express, “Elements of Surprise”, 1/7/5, http://www.tnr.com/doc.mhtml?i=express&s=easterbrook010705, [CL] the comet usually blamed for the dinosaurs' disappearance struck with such violence that it cracked tectonic plates,
We have an ethical obligation to spend the money to colonize space – even if extinction is not probable, the minute possibility makes space colonies a requirement
Shapiro 2k7 (Robert Shapiro is Professor Emeritus and Senior Research Scientist in the Chemistry Department of New York University. March 19, 2007, "Why the moon? Human survival!”, http://www.thespacereview.com/article/832/1) we do not need to invoke them to provide reasons for our investment
Timeframe is irrelevant – in order to solve problems like space colonization, we have to take a long view of impacts.
Brand 99 (Stewart, Futurist, President of the Long Now Foundation, “The Clock of the Long Now” ) is the longer view you take, the more is in your self-interest
Magnitude outweighs timeframe – not acting against potential threats equates moral evasion
Oberg 98[Congressional Testifier on the Russian Space Program, NASA International Space Station Contributor, Space Studies Expert / James, “ASTEROID DEFLECTION & THE FUTURE OF HUMAN INTERVENTION IN THE EARTH'S BIOSPHERE”, Futures Focus Day Symposium sponsored by the US Space Command/] We know why the dinosaurs died out. It was because they didn't have a space program
Extinction first – there is a categorical difference between existential and non-existential threats
Matheny 7 [Jason G., Prof of Health Policy and Management, Bloomberg School of Public Health at Johns Hopkins University, “Reducing the Risk of Human Extinction,” Risk Analysis, v27n5]Even though the risk to any existing individual of dying in a car crash is much greater than the risk of dying in an asteroid impact, asteroids pose a much greater risk
And, nuclear war doesn’t cause extinction
Seitz 6[Russell is a Visiting Scholar in Harvard University's Center for International Affairs, published in Nature and Foreign Affairs on the subject of nuclear winter, Dec 20, “The Nuclear Winter Meltdown: Photoshopping the Apocalypse” adamant.typepad.com/seitz/2006/12/preherein_honor.html ] simulations that they do not reproduce the apocalyptic results

Only nuclear pulse propulsion can solve for the infrastructure to colonize space
Smith 3[the founder of NuclearSpace (Wayne, “The Case for Orion”, Space Daily, http://www.spacedaily.com/news/nuclearspace-03h.html] What Orion can do is put unimaginably massive payloads into space at low cost.

NASA would build Orion if they had the nukes
Bernstein 2 [Professor Emeritus at the Stevens Institute of Technology (Jeremy, “Project Orion: The True Story of the Atomic Spaceship”, Book Review, American Journal of Physics they are once again looking into Orion-type vehicles that might be assembled in space

No counterplan will solve space
Ragheb ‘9, Associate Professor of Nuclear, Plasma, and Radiological Engineering (Magdi, “Nuclear Pulse and Plasma Space Propulsion”,https://netfiles.uiuc.edu/mragheb/www/NPRE%20402%20ME%20405%20Nuclear%20Power%20Engineering/Nuclear%20and%20Plasma%20Space%20Propulsion.pdf)External pulse systems possess higher temperature limits and lower inert masses and circumvent that limitation
