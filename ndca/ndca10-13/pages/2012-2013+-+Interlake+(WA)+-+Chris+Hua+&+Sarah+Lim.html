Introduction

The primary purpose of this wiki is to share tips and tricks about creating ideal coleslaw. As the curator of this wiki is not vegan, some tips and tricks will be specific to creating vegan coleslaw, but most will be universal.

A secondary purpose of this wiki is to share interesting citations.
This should be a space for cabbage and coleslaw enthusiasts who come from all walks of life.



Tip #1: Use salt to prevent excess wetness.
Remember to salt the coleslaw so that it doesn't get too wet
just remember the 4 S's
1) SALT the cabbage
2) STIR the salt into the cabbage
3) Let SIT for a few hours
4) SPIN to remove excess wetness
