Racism AC


Violent Juvenile offenders are being treated in the Juvenile court system in some states.

Campbell 2k10, Jacob. "Juveniles Involved in the Juvenile Justice System Article | Jacob R. Campbell." Jacob R. Campbell's Website | Jacob R. Campbell. 31 Aug. 2010. Web. 31 Dec. 2010. <http://jacobrcampbell.com/resources/juveniles-involved-juvenile-justice-system-article>.


Put away your “Adult system is racist” cards, if I can prove that the juvenile system is comparatively worse, then I should win the round. With that being said -

The Juvenile system is more likely to foster racism than the adult system – Star this argument

Scott & Steinberg 2k8 Elizabeth S. Scott, & Laurence Steinberg, Law Professor-Columbia & Psychology Professor-Temple, 2008, Rethinking Juvenile Justice, p. 225-6



Racist interaction within the mechanisms of the Juvenile Justice System increases deviance in youths of color

Conley ’94 [Darlene, Prof. Sociology Univ. Washington, May 1994; Journal of Research in Crime and Delinquency, “Adding color to a black and white picture: using qualitative data to explain racial disproportionality in the juvenile justice system; pg 145]


Remember when I told you to put away those cards? Yeah, well I would just caution against running a Negative Case or Disad against this too – we root cause all of your violence impacts.

Foucault '76 [Michel, Society Must be Defended: Lectures at the College de France, 1975-1976, p. 254-257 Trans. David Macey


Racist legal orderings erodes confidence in the legal system.

The Sentencing Project 2k10. "Reducing Racial Disparity in the Criminal Justice System." 2010. Web. 31 Dec. 2010. <http://www.sentencingproject.org/doc/publications/rd_reducingracialdisparity.pdf>.


Erosion of confidence in the court systems kills democracy and political stability
Newton ‘99, Kenneth. "Confidence in Public Institutions." Harvard University, Sept. 1999. Web. 31 Dec. 2010. <http://www.hks.harvard.edu/fs/pnorris/Acrobat/NEWTON.PDF>.


Democracy solves environment – less war, famines and more repsonsiblity.
Li & Reuveny 2k7 Li, Prof of poli sci at Penn State, and Reuveny, prof of public and environmental affairs @ Indiana U 07
(Quan and Rafael, “The Effects of Liberalism on the Terrestrial Environment” http://cmp.sagepub.com/cgi/content/abstract/24/3/219)


While I recognize that the 1AC cannot solve for all racism, my in round discourse is the first step that must be taken; it is a necessary precondition for stopping racism not only in the juvenile justice system but also in its other forms.
The Sentencing Project 2k10. "Reducing Racial Disparity in the Criminal Justice System." 2010. Web. 31 Dec. 2010. <http://www.sentencingproject.org/doc/publications/rd_reducingracialdisparity.pdf>.


Racism must be rejected when given the chance
Barndt ‘99, [Joseph, co-director of Crossroads a ministry to dismantle racism, 1991, “ Dismantling Racism: The Countinuing challenge to White America”, pg 155-6.]


The ballot must be used to stop the harming of the other at all other costs. It is the only discursive option available to take the crucial step towards solving our impact scenarios. The role of the ballot is to affirm whoever creates the best forum of change in favor of the irreducible other.
