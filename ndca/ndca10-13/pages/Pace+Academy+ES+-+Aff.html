Harvard Round Robin

Plan: The United States federal government should substantially increase its Highway Trust Fund transportation infrastructure investment by increasing the federal excise tax rate and phasing in a price floor and variable tax on gasoline.

St. Mark's

Plan: The United States federal government should substantially increase its Highway Trust Fund surface transportation infrastructure investment by increasing the federal excise tax rate and phasing in a price floor and variable tax on gasoline.

Greenhill Round Robin

Plan: The United States federal government should substantially increase its Highway Trust Fund highway investment by increasing the federal excise tax rate and phasing in a price floor and variable tax on gasoline.
