Advantage 1 is Orientalism

In the 1980s my parents immigrated to America along with thousands of other Hangukins. The fact that they immigrated shows the effect of colonialism on Hangukins. When the Americans came into Daehanminguk they caused our people to have a mindset that America is better than them. This would cause our people to look to America for everything and for us to become a vulnerable state power in the world. In fact it was just the effects of Western Orientalism and colonialism that caused my country to be split into Daehanminguk and Bukhan. When the Americans came into our country that split our country of Daehan Joseon with their paranoia building up the communist party of Joseon and causing the split into Bukhan and Daehanminguk. They have caused bloodshed and dehumanized millions of people in the region and allowing their continuation in Daehanminguk concedes to wrong idea that it's okay to use violent colonialized and orientalist language. It's not only an insult to me and my people but it's something that affects everyone. Everyday when people use orientalist and colonialist discourse on policy issues on Daehanminguk. I feel dehumanized because I am looked as a subject of orientalism and colonialism. The only way to stop further dehumanization is to change the way we talk about Daehanminguk as intellectuals and future policymakers. This will cause a spillover effect where we never have to be subject to orientalist or colonialist language again. Because we are discussing this in this round, we start a cycle to stop Orientalism and colonialism.

Thus, Priyanka and I advocate that the United States Federal Government should withdraw military presence from Daehanminguk
