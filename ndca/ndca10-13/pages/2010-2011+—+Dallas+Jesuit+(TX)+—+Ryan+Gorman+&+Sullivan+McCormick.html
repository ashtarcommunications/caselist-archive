Genealogy 1aC


After debating Japan for an entire debate season, Sullivan and I have concluded that we have talked about the implications of our military presence in Japan without understanding the origins of that presence. A reflection on the underlying justifications of our occupation of Japan is required to understand a withdrawal of our military and police presence in Japan.
