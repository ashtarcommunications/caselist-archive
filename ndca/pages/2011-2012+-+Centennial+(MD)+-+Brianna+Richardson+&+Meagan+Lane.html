On first glance the resolution seemed to be another chance for the society to influence imperialism and white supremacy in the minds of youth. However, after viewing the resolution in an afrocentric viewpoint, My partner and I realized that topics about space are the best topic for which we can begin exploring beyond the current structure of debate. This metaphor allows us to explore the “unknown” that is barely ever spoken of in policy debate

The Purpose:
SUBSTANTIATE MEANINGFUL EPISTEMOLOGICAL GROWTH
We pedagogically prioritize purpose allowing epistemological growth to ALTER the social structure. Moving BEYOND the limits of WINS/LOSSES, we strive to make the WIN matter, to make debate matter, to DEVELOP self through the exploration of the discussion that happen within debate rounds. My blackness is a process that NEVER ends an ongoing dynamic that is fostered within the process of debate, I AM BLACK, I AM DEBATE
OUR DISCUSSION OF THE TOPIC BEGINS WITH A REINTERPRETATION OF SPACE

Space is the unknown that offers the potential of unlimited benefits a chance to start over once we have exhausted the possibilities of exploitation that are harnessed within our grasp.
MILLS Associate Prof of Philosophy @ U Illinois, Chicago 1997
Charles-; The Racial Contract; p. 1-3



RESOLVED:

THE UNITED STATES FEDERAL GOVERNMENT SHOULD INCREASE ITS SPACE EXPLORATION AND OR DEVELOPMENT BEYOND THE EARTH’S MESOPHERE

JUST LIKE

DECISIONMAKERS THAT REPRESENT OUR COMMUNITY SHOULD OPEN UP THE PEDAGOGICAL POSSIBILITIES THAT GO BEYOND THE LIMITS OF THE CURRENT STRUCTURES OF KNOWLEDGE.

A metaphorical interpretation of the resolution solves best. It allows us as debaters to regain our agency and stop engaging in the collective hallucination of fiat. Like Elizabeth Jones says: My words right here, right now can’t change the state, but they can change the state of debate.
Dr. Reid-Brinkley, 2008[Shanara Rose, Professor of African American Studies at Pittsburg University, “The Harsh Realities of ‘Acting Black’”: How African America Policy Debaters Negotiate Representation Through Racial Performance And Style, C.A.]


OUR SPEAKING STYLE IS IMPORTANT TO THE EVALUATION OF THE DEBATE
è Language functions as a model of one’s culture. Black language is an elaborate and unique speaking style that emphasizes performance and relies heavily on the use of Metaphors rejecting the SOLE reliance on logic and evidence. The use of black language is our defiance to the TOTAL INDOCTRINATION of white America.
Our metaphorical interpretation of the resolution is an active criticism that serves to liberate us by embracing and preserving unique Black culture.
HILL prof @ BIOLA University 1997
Shelton K. -; African American Students’ Motivation to Participate in intercollegiate Debate; THE SOUTHERN JOURNAL OF FORENSICS 2 Fall 202-235;


Play "Spaceship" – Kanye West
Kanye shows his own experience. He points out that the oppression is giving him the urge to “grab a spaceship and fly”. Likewise, the oppression of our society has given us the urge to escape the bounds of white supremacy and dive into the unknown of afrofuturism.


The topic calls for us to debate space exploration. In debate this is science fiction because we fictionally pretend to be a government that will not change anything while debating the science of space. Science fiction needs to be retaken by the black subject through a process of Afro-futurism. How can a community whose history has been deliberately erased by white supremacy? By using afro futurism to place themselves outside of the system of white supremacy

2AC A2: Framework

A. Counter-interpretation – View the resolution as a metaphor.
Answer Topicality with Framework. We don’t agree with the topicality of the resolution, thus we advocate a metaphorical interpretation. Our framework solves through the methodological approach. Through this approach we gain the epistemological growth that is required to debate.
B. Role of the Ballot- We don’t roleplay. We debate to gain epistemological growth. We signify correct methodology to ending white supremacy. We leave the round gaining epistemological growth.
C. Uniqueness – We control the direction or decision making. We don’t need movements or people. Brianna and I – together – can gain the epistemological growth and create a methodology that effectively fights white supremacy.
D. Our interpretation solves best for the debate round. We take out their argument by destroying their internal link. They may say that it’s not predictable, but they have no risk of their predictability arguments, for two reasons:
