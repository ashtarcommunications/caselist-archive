Affirmative


The value is Morality.

Ought is defined as desirability.

Since societal definition of desirability hinges on what is right and wrong, which is dictated to us through Morality, it must be the paramount value in this round.

Observation 1: The resolution does not mandate that the affirmative defend the discontinuation of drug use.

Observation 2: the negative must defend stopping drugs; they must advocate criminalization. The affirmative must also state that criminalization is a bad policy, as well as the fact that public health is a good standard for the round.

The Criterion is minimizing suffering.

Since suffering is a direct violation of a moral side constraint, it is going to link back into the value through both deontology, and most forms of utilitarianism, since it is not the greatest good if everyone is suffering.
