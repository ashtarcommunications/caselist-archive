C. Standards:

The level of American involvement in an area in which an opportunity takes place is perhaps the most visible demonstration of US commitment. US military involvement as defined includes: (1) an established American military presence, defined as a U.S. military base, (2) the furnishing of military aid to some state or organization, or (3) a prior use of force. Such investment represents American interest and obligation to allies, enemies, and neutral parties alike.

1.
