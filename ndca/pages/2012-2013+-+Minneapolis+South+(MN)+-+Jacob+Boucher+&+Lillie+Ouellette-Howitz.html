Hypotesting/Trains Aff


Contention One is Railways

Throughout my childhood I rode rails across the country with my family, first when we took a family trip to Chicago on this trip I only had a short interaction with trains but already I knew that I loved them, then again I rode a train across the country to bring my sister to college in MA, on this trip I was able to see more of the country and my excitement grew, then finally I took a trip to Seattle by rails, on this trip I was able to see the other coast of the US and my enthusiasm for trains was solidified. These experiences showed me first hand what the transportation system of railways throughout the united states is all about and gave me a great appreciation and love of our railways.

Trains are my childhood, and that’s why we need them.

For Jacob, trains also started at an early age. When he was little, he had a set of trains built for him by his Grandfather. From that time on, he knew that trains were some of his favorite toys. Thomas the tank engine was the #1 show to watch, and was necessary if you were to truly experience a day in the Boucher household. He would spend hours watching and playing with his trains, it brought his family together for “Train Time.” It helped create a bond with his family that opened the opportunity to learn and grow. Just as they were to me, trains were a crucial piece of Jacob’s childhood.

Thus, the United States federal government should substantially increase its transportation infrastructure investment in the United States.
