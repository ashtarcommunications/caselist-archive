Privatization CP


Text: The United States federal government should create a government organization separate from NASA to manage space prizes and authorize that organization to fundraise. That organization should issue a substantial monetary prize for the first private company that develops and deploys Space Based Solar Power, provided that that company shares any new technology or data with the National Aeronautics and Space Administration. The United States federal government should give that organization operational authority over launches, space missions, and space-related patents but also mandate the ability for the military to utilize any technology that is developed. The United States federal government should remove any current regulations and not impose any new regulations on the private space industry.
