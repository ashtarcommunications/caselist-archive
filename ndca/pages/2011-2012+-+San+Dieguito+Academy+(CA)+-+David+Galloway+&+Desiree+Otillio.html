Plan

The United States federal government should fully fund and launch the space shuttle program.



Contention 1 – International Space Station

The ISS will atrophy absent a renewed US shuttle program – other countries can’t solve

Augustine ‘9, Norman (Chairman of the U.S. Human Spaceflight Plans Committee). 2009. ("SEEKING A HUMAN SPACEFLIGHT PROGRAM WORTHY OF A GREAT NATION." 2 Review of U.S. Human Spaceflight Plans Committee (2009): 54. Web. <legislative.nasa.gov/396093main_HSF_Cmte_Final Report.pdf>.)


Continued maintenance of the ISS key – 2 reasons


First, the ISS is critical to space colonization-- it provides the only means to test life support technologies in space
