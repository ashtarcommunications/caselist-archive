Plan text we have read

Thus we offer the following plan: The United States federal government should substantially reduce its B61 and troops stationed for the upkeep of B61 to zero in Anatolia. We reserve the right to clarify.

Thus we offer the following plan: the United States federal government should substantially reduce its Tactical Nuclear Forces from Anatolia to zero. We reserve the right to clarify.

Thus we offer the following plan: United States federal government should gradually but completely reduce its military presence form Anatolia. We reserve the right to clarify.

Thus we offer the following plan: the president of the United States federal government should issue an executive order to substantially reduce its military presence form Anatolia. We reserve the right to clarify.

Thus we offer the following plan: the United States federal government should gradually but completely reduce its B61 gravity bombs and troops in the Republic of Turkey to zero.

Thus we offer the following plan: the United States federal government should substantially reduce its thermonuclear weapons within the geographical location of Anatolia. We reserve the right to clarify.

Thus we offer the following plan: the United States federal government should phase out a substantial portion of its tactical nuclear presence in Anatolia.
