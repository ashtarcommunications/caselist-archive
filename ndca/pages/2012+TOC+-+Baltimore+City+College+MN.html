A hegemonic neoliberalism utilizing nation building, party politics, the military industrial complex, and a fundamental motive of profit are increasingly the drive of the US into space. This is characterized by an increasing privatization and deregulation of space interests, increasingly foreclosing indigent access.
Parker 9 (Martin Parker is Professor of Culture and Organization at the University of Leicester School of Management, Space Travel & Culture: from Apollo to space tourism, edited by David Bell and Martin Parker, Available online: http://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.1111/sore.2009.57.issue-s1/issuetoc, The Editorial Board of The Sociological ReviewDN)
