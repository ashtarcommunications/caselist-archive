Affirmative


In this debate we will defend the following: The United States Federal Government should substantially reduce its military and police presence by permanently eliminating all its present and future military and police presence in Okinawa.
Contention One is Coloniality:
The Eurocentered colonization of the globe imposed a system of classification, knowledge and power that imposed a system of gender, defined by conquerors, on the rest of the world, in order to facilitate conquest and initiate enduring systems of coloniality –
Lugones 07
(Maria, director of the Center for Interdisciplinary Studies in Philosophy, Interpretation, and Culture at SUNY Binghamton, “Heterosexualism and the Colonial / Modern Gender System.” Hypatia 22.1, pp. 186-7.)
Understanding the place of gender …these are matters of resistance to domination.

Coloniality demands an inverted logic of identity where the colonizer’s evils are projected onto gendered and racialized bodies that are condemned to the hell of perpetual violence and control. Ethics are permanently suspended and normal life for members of colonizing societies is driven by a death ethic of conquest, rape, and killing.
Maldonado-Torres 08
(Nelson, assoc. prof Ethnic Studies at UC Berkeley, Against War: Views from the Underside of Modernity, p. 216-221)
