Aff Run at Districts:



1AC

One of the most important forms of transportation in the US is waterways. Investing in the restoration of river infrastructures, to make them better for transportation of boating for recreation and fishing, is our way of upholding this year’s resolution – that the United States federal government should substantially increase its investment in transportation infrastructure. Therefore we offer the following plan.

The United States federal government should invest in the removal of the following federally funded dams: Ice Harbor, Lower Monumental, Little Goose, and Lower Granite.
