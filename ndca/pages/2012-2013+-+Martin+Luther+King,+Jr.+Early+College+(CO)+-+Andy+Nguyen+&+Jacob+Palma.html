PLAN
The Affirmative team presents the following plan: the United States Federal Government should substantially increase its transportation infrastructure investment in the United States by immediately

(a) targeting investment block grants at “structurally deficient” bridges, and
(b) making it illegal for states to reallocate federal bridge maintenance and rebuilding funds, and
(c) incentivizing states to keep bridges in good repair.
