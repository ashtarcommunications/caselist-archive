Negative-Case

"OMG Nuclear War!"

The Aff uses the idea of wars and extinction in order to side-step issues of racism and oppression. Words like Nuke War make you think we are in jeopardy however, people of color have lost their culture for centuries and live in constant war every day.
Barbara Omolade, 1984“Women of Color and the Nuclear Holocaust” [Sociologist and educator Dr. Barbara Omolade received her bachelor’s degree from Queens College, her master’s degree from Goddard College, and her PhD from the City University of New York. She has worked extensively for women’s and activist organizations and in academia]
Military terror has been used to destroy resistance to racism......have already been declared on our jobs, our housing, our schools, our families and our lands.

Western civilization is a dysfunctional one which attempts to justify atrocities
Cesaire 72(discourse on colonialism 1972 original publish in 55 pg: 1 Aime Cesaire, attended the Lycée Louis-le Grand, the École Normale Supérieure, and ultimately the Sorbonne G.L)
A civilization that uses its principles for trickery and deceit is a dying civilization...... lets us go straight to the principal lie which is the source of all the others.

"State Good/Inevitable"

The affirmative’s call for a short term policy reform culturally and politically normalizes the explicit and openly racist technologies of state violence that have neutralized entire populations of our people. The only way to solve is thru an abolitionist politics
Rodriguez, 2008 (Dylan, Professor and Chair of the Department of Ethnic Studies at UC Riverside, “Warfare and the Terms of Engagement”,C.A.)
Not long from now, generations will emerge from.....the state can and must be radically confronted on multiple fronts by an abolitionist politics.
